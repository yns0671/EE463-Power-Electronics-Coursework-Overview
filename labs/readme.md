# Laboratory Experiments – EE463 Power Electronics I

This folder contains laboratory reports and measurement results from **EE463 – Power Electronics I**.  
The experiments focus on hands-on investigation of power electronic circuits, devices, and systems, complementing analytical and simulation-based coursework.

## Covered Experiments

### Experiment 1 – Power Semiconductor Device Characterization
- Familiarization with power electronics laboratory equipment  
- Diode and thyristor V–I characteristics  
- Forward conduction behavior and switching-related observations  

### Experiment 2 – Single- and Three-Phase Diode Rectifiers
- Single-phase full-bridge rectifier analysis  
- Three-phase Y and Δ connected rectifier configurations  
- Power quality metrics, harmonic analysis, and filtering effects  

### Experiment 3 – DC–DC Converters
- Buck, boost, and buck–boost converter operation  
- CCM and DCM behavior  
- Inductor characteristics, synchronous switching, soft-start, and dynamic response  

### Experiment 4 – Transformer Design and Flyback Converter
- High-frequency transformer design and inductance measurements  
- Leakage and mutual inductance analysis  
- Multi-output flyback converter operation and regulation behavior
